Good for Commuters:
  • Sharing costs saves money
  • Insurance costs can go down
  • Make better use of your commuting time
  • Help protect the environment
More Information on Share-A-Ride
Good for Employers
  • Encouraging shared commutes can save a componay money on parking leases or maintenance and insurance
  • Your employees will get to work on time more often, and take fewer sick days (really!)
  • You show your employees, and the community, you are environmentally friendly

If you would like assistance with implementing commute alternatives for your work site, contact the Transportation Management Association (TMA) for your region.
